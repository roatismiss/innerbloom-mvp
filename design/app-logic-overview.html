A non-technical walkthrough

An emotionally-intelligent social network for young Filipinos navigating their inner life.

Most apps reward performance. InnerBloom rewards honesty. People check in with how they actually feel, and the app responds — with quiet content, kindred company, and a gentle AI companion.

Pilot · Metro Manila & Cebu · 2026 React Native · iOS & Android · Web preview Supabase · Realtime · GPT-4o 5 core experiences · 14 screens · 1 backend
The Idea

One feeling in. A whole afternoon of care out.

Every day a person taps how they feel — anxious, sad, stressed, neutral, happy, hopeful — and that single signal becomes the spine of the experience. From it, the app shapes the quotes they read, the reels they swipe, the strangers they meet, and the tone the AI uses with them. The mood check-in is not a side feature. It is the entire engine.

The engine

One mood becomes five personalized rooms.

When the user submits today's check-in, a single backend function — today_for_me() — fans the signal out to every surface. The user feels it as serendipity. It's really a deliberate, mood-aware composition.

A
User picks a mood
Six categories × intensity 1–5 × one anchor word. Stored as a single row in mood_checkins.
B
Streak trigger fires
Database trigger recomputes consecutive-day streak and writes to mood_streaks.
C
today_for_me() runs
Picks 1 quote, 6 reels, a feed slice, and a focus prompt that match the mood.
D
Soul Match pool refreshes
User is now eligible for today's match window. Quiz responses join a pool keyed on date + feeling + energy.
E
Bloom AI inherits the tone
The companion's system prompt reads today's mood and softens or steadies its register accordingly.
How it's built (in plain English)

A thin app on top of a quiet, opinionated backend.

Three layers. Each does one job well.

Layer 1 What the user sees
A React Native app (Expo) that ships to iPhone, Android, and a web preview from a single codebase. Designed to feel literary — Fraunces serif for tender moments, Nunito Sans for clarity, warm peach & cyan palette.
Layer 2 The brain & the memory
Supabase stores everything — profiles, moods, posts, matches, messages — and enforces who can see what via Row-Level Security. Database triggers handle streaks, resonance counts, and the daily mood-to-content fan-out automatically, with no manual jobs to babysit.
Layer 3 The companion's voice
Two small Edge Functions sit alongside the database: bloom-chat calls GPT-4o with a carefully written system prompt and persists every exchange; find-soul-match resolves the day's compatibility pool. Both are stateless and isolated — easy to swap, monitor, or replace later.

A suggested sequence

What to build, in what order, to reach a pilot-ready MVP.

Three two-week sprints. The order is deliberate: unblock the core social loop first (matching + messaging), then make it safe and discoverable, then polish for the stores.

Sprint 1
Close the social loop · weeks 1–2
  1. Wire Supabase Realtime to the Soul Match conversation screen.
  2. Schedule find-soul-match as a daily cron so every active user gets exactly one pairing per day.
  3. Seed the first batch of bloom_quotes (~80) and bloom_reels (~60) so feeds aren't empty.
  4. Wire Expo Push: match-found, new message, daily nudge.
Sprint 2
Make it safe & honest · weeks 3–4
  1. Crisis safety net: intensity-5 distress & trigger-word detection routes to Philippines helplines.
  2. Reporting & moderation: report button on posts & messages, admin review queue, soft-mute.
  3. Email verification + password reset flow on the auth screen.
  4. Profile media: avatar upload via Supabase Storage.
  5. Empty & error states on every screen that fetches data.
Sprint 3
Ship to the pilot · weeks 5–6
  1. Analytics: PostHog or Amplitude — funnels for onboarding, check-in, match, message.
  2. App icon, splash, App Store / Play Store screenshots, listing copy.
  3. Privacy policy + terms of service (Philippines-specific, plain Filipino-English).
  4. EAS build pipeline → TestFlight + Play Internal Testing → 50-person pilot in Metro Manila & Cebu.
  5. Daily review of the first week's analytics; weekly content top-up.
