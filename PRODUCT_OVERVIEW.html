What is InnerBloom?

A compassionate mental wellness companion that combines daily mindfulness practices, emotional tracking, and supportive community circles.

InnerBloom is more than an app—it's a gentle space where you can pause, reflect, and grow. Whether you're navigating anxiety, seeking connection, or simply wanting to understand yourself better, InnerBloom walks alongside you with evidence-based practices, AI-powered insights, and a community that truly understands.

Core Features

📝

Daily Intentions

Set 3 meaningful intentions each morning. Track your "To-Be List" throughout the day, and reflect each evening on whether you honored your commitments. Earn points for consistency and self-awareness.

😊

Mood Tracking

Check in daily with 5 mood levels (Radiant, Good, Steady, Tired, Low). Visualize your emotional patterns with beautiful mood calendars and trend charts. Unlock insights about what influences your wellbeing over time.

📖

Emotional Journal

A private space for free-form reflection. Write as much or as little as you need. Your entries are analyzed for patterns—gratitude, stress, joy—giving you weekly insights powered by AI to help you understand your emotional landscape.

🎯

Gamification & Rewards

Build streaks for daily check-ins, complete mindfulness quests, and earn points for honoring your intentions. Track your journey with a visual garden that blooms as you grow. Celebrate small wins that compound into lasting change.

🤖

Bloom AI Companion

Your 24/7 mental wellness companion. Ask questions, process emotions, or simply chat when you need support. Bloom AI provides personalized affirmations, coping strategies, and gentle guidance grounded in therapeutic principles.

🎬

Mindfulness Reels

Scroll through bite-sized wisdom—quotes, affirmations, and daily blooms set to calming ambient music. Save favorites, share with matched souls, and let inspiration find you in moments of need. Like TikTok, but for your inner peace.

💫

Soul Match

Connect with kindred spirits through a thoughtful matching process. Answer questions about your journey, values, and needs. Get matched with 1-3 souls who truly resonate. Share reels, discuss topics, and build authentic friendships in a safe space.

🫂

Support Circles

Join topic-based communities: Anxiety Support, Depression, Grief, Recovery, Mindfulness. Share anonymously or as yourself. Access relief toolkits (breathing exercises, grounding techniques), voice notes from others, and real-time pulse checks showing how the circle feels today.

🧘

Guided Practices

Box breathing (4-7-8 technique), body scan meditations, and grounding exercises. Each practice is designed to be completed in under 10 minutes—perfect for busy days when you need a moment to reset and recenter.

How It Works: Your Daily Journey

🌅 Morning (5 minutes) Open InnerBloom and check in with your mood. Set your primary intention for the day and add 2-3 small actions to your "To-Be List." These aren't tasks—they're ways of being (e.g., "I will move with patience," "I will listen deeply").
☀️ Throughout the Day Check off your To-Be actions as you honor them. Open the app when you need support— chat with Bloom AI, watch a mindfulness reel, or practice a 4-minute breathing exercise. Your progress is automatically tracked.
🌙 Evening (5 minutes) Reflect on your day. Did you honor your intention? Write a journal entry if you feel called. Review your mood trend and see your weekly insights. Celebrate your consistency— every check-in builds your streak and earns points.
🤝 Anytime Connection Join a support circle when you need community. Share a post (anonymously or openly), listen to voice notes from others, or send group warmth. Connect with your matched souls through shared reels and meaningful conversations.
